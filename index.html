How this estimate works and what is not exact
Measured inputs

Maximum S00 HP, ATK, DEF, Combat Power, active-skill multipliers, faction data, and configured passive text from the extracted game data.

APK-informed rules

Higher total CP acts first. Four units begin On Field, but each side turn selects a maximum of three primary command cards; passive-triggered skills and pursuits are extra actions. Slot 5 enters from Backup on the next turn after a defeat. The model audits active skills, equipped natural-gift/rank passives, and each unit's UR Ability Sphere, mapping APK events including side-turn, card release, damage, hit, healing, death, Weak, Power Burst, and Backup triggers.

Modeled behavior

Formation slots set initial ATK priority: Slot 1 uses Team Max +6%, Slot 2 +4%, Slot 3 +2%, and Slot 4 Team Max. This controls opening action order and highest-initial-ATK passive targeting; later battle buffs or debuffs can change effective ATK order. Active-skill levels use their configured multipliers and entries. Unit attributes follow the APK rule: STR > SKL > SPD > STR, PSY/WIT counter each other, and DST counters all; advantage/disadvantage changes damage by ±20%.

Not yet exact

The result now identifies partial and unreproducible passives instead of silently ignoring them. Named-status state machines, exact card-hand operations, encrypted runtime tie-breaking, account bonuses, and server-only timing remain approximations.
